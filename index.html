There is a two players/coins Red and yellow. Get four or more of your coins in a row (horizontally, vertically or diagonally), Whoever can do it first will win. Click on the column in which want to place the coin. At first you will find Red and New game button. When turn of Red coin Red button will appear and turn of Yellow coin Yellow button will appear. You can also click the red and yellow button to pick a random column, remember it is not an Ai (artificial intelligence).
